Roast Spatchcock Turkey


Description

Whip up a batch of this decadent slow cooker creamed corn and discover the joy of effortless cooking combined with mouth-watering flavors.
This recipe is not only a breeze to make but also rich in nutrients, including antioxidants like lutein and zeaxanthin, which promote healthy vision.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon granulates sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients: In your slow cooker, mix the corn, milk, cream, butter, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook: Set your slow cooker on low and let the concoction cook for 3 to 4 hours. This slow cooking process allows the flavors to meld beautifully.
  3. Final Touch: Before serving, stir everything well. For a thicker consistency, you can blend a portion of the corn mixture and stir it back in.
